10 Steps to Master DSA

  1. Understand the fundamentals of computer science (memory, CPU, I/O).
  2. Learn the basics of programming (variables, loops, functions).
  3. Choose a programming language (C++, Java, Python, etc.).
  4. Study core data structures (arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues).
  5. Practice basic algorithms (searching, sorting, recursion).
  6. Master advanced data structures (trees, heaps, graphs, hash tables).
  7. Understand time and space complexity (Big O notation).
  8. Solve problems regularly on coding platforms (LeetCode, Codeforces, etc.).
  9. Learn problem-solving patterns (sliding window, two pointers, divide and conquer).
  10. Participate in contests and mock interviews to test your skills.
